Anthropic Overtakes OpenAI Valuation

Anthropic surpassed OpenAI to become the world’s most valuable AI startup after raising $65 billion in a Series H funding round. AI ethics Report 02

Pope Challenges AI Salvation Narratives

Pope Leo XIV’s first encyclical strongly criticizes technological messianism, warning against treating artificial intelligence as humanity’s ultimate solution. The document argues that unchecked AI development risks concentrating power, deepening inequality, weakening human relationships, and undermining worker dignity. Leo compares current technological ambitions to a modern Tower of Babel, emphasizing the dangers of replacing human judgment with automated systems. He urges governments, technology companies, and society to establish ethical safeguards, democratic oversight, and limits on autonomous decision-making. The encyclical also highlights concerns surrounding surveillance, warfare, labor displacement, and misinformation, positioning human dignity and moral responsibility above technological acceleration.

network vulnerability Report 03

PAN-OS VPN Bypass Exploited

Palo Alto Networks warned that attackers are actively exploiting a PAN-OS GlobalProtect authentication bypass vulnerability tracked as CVE-2026-0257. The flaw allows unauthorized users to establish VPN connections without valid credentials when specific GlobalProtect authentication override configurations are enabled. Researchers observed exploitation attempts beginning in mid-May, targeting exposed and unpatched firewall deployments. Successful attacks provide network access that could facilitate lateral movement, reconnaissance, and further compromise of internal systems. Palo Alto and security researchers urged organizations to apply patches immediately, review VPN configurations, restrict unnecessary exposure, and monitor authentication activity for suspicious connections and unauthorized access attempts.
