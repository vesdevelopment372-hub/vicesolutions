Monthly Threat Snapshot — February 2026

Prepared by Vic E Solutions Security Research Team

Overview

This month we observed a significant increase in ransomware targeting small and medium-sized enterprises across India. Attackers are leveraging credential harvesting, phishing kits, and exposed RDP services.

Key Threats Identified

  • Ransomware campaigns using phishing attachments
  • Credential stuffing attacks on SaaS platforms
  • Exposed cloud storage misconfigurations
  • Outdated VPN appliances exploited

Recommended Actions

  • Enable multi-factor authentication across all services
  • Patch critical vulnerabilities immediately
  • Conduct employee phishing awareness training
  • Deploy endpoint detection and response (EDR)

Conclusion

Organizations should adopt a proactive cybersecurity posture and perform regular security assessments to mitigate emerging risks. Vic E Solutions continues to monitor the evolving threat landscape.
